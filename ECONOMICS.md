# ECONOMICS

If Credex deployed this AI Spend Audit tool tomorrow, the core business model would be lead generation for AI optimization consulting and financial products related to startup spending.

A converted lead could realistically be worth between $300–$1500 depending on company size and follow-up services purchased.

For example:

* A startup reducing AI SaaS waste may later purchase broader expense optimization services.
* A founder discovering duplicated subscriptions may be interested in financial tooling or consulting.
* Teams using multiple enterprise AI plans could become high-value recurring customers.

Assume:

* Average customer value: $1000
* Gross margin after servicing: 70%
* Effective value per converted customer: ~$700

If the audit-to-paid conversion rate is 2%, then every 100 completed audits produce roughly 2 customers.

That means:

* 100 audits → 2 customers
* 2 customers × $700 effective value = $1400 expected value per 100 audits
* Approximate value per completed audit = $14

The CAC model depends heavily on acquisition channel.

Organic founder outreach:

* Estimated CAC: $5–$20
* Mostly time-based cost

Community distribution:

* Estimated CAC: near $0 initially
* Requires strong product positioning

SEO/content:

* CAC low long-term
* Expensive in time upfront

The funnel assumptions for profitability could look like this:

* 1000 visitors
* 300 audits completed
* 45 report submissions
* 10 consultation calls
* 2 paid conversions

If each conversion generates roughly $1000 in downstream value, the economics become attractive even at relatively low conversion rates.

For this tool to drive $1M ARR within 18 months, several things would need to happen:

* Strong organic distribution in founder communities
* Expansion beyond audits into subscription management or benchmarking
* High-value B2B consulting upsells
* Recurring SaaS reporting features

Example math:

* 1000 paying companies
* Average revenue per company: $100/month
* Monthly recurring revenue: $100,000
* ARR: $1.2M

This would require the tool evolving from a lightweight audit calculator into a recurring AI spend management platform with analytics, alerts, and benchmarking features.
