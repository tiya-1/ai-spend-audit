# FAQ

## 1. What does this tool do?

The AI Spend Audit analyzes your current AI subscriptions and recommends lower-cost plans or optimizations based on team size and usage patterns.

## 2. Which AI tools are supported?

Currently supported tools include ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, Windsurf, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API.

## 3. Is my data stored?

Only minimal audit and lead information is stored for generating recommendations and improving the product experience.

## 4. How are savings calculated?

Savings are estimated using publicly available pricing data from official vendor pricing pages combined with your selected team and plan information.

## 5. Is this meant for enterprises only?

No. The tool is designed for freelancers, startups, indie hackers, and small engineering teams as well.
